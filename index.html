Projects

M1911 A1 Custom Version 3D Model

Project description: A brief introduction to the background, technologies used, and results.

A 3D model of the classic M1911 A1 pistol for 3D printing enthusiasts, ready for download and printing.

Modeled using Blender based on engineering drawings, with detailed refinement of every component.

Started in October 2024 and completed in February 2025, taking 4 months and a total of 144 hours.

A companion animation simulation was specially made to demonstrate the firing process.

(PGB) Portable Growing Box

Project description: A brief introduction to the background, technologies used, and results.

A portable growing box concept to address potential food supply issues caused by extreme weather conditions for urban residents.

Enables urban users to grow vegetables at home without relying on large-scale agriculture.

Started in December 2024 and currently in the prototype stage, expected to be completed by the end of 2026.

After successful feasibility testing, an open-source hardware plan will be released to involve more enthusiasts.
